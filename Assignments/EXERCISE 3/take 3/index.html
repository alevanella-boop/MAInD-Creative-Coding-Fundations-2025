Tic-tac-toe is a two-player game on a 3x3 grid where players alternate placing an 'X' or 'O' in an empty square. The goal is to be the first player to get three of their marks in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. If all nine squares are filled and no player has three in a row, the game is a tie.
